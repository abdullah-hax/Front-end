Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a beautiful and culturally rich country located in South Asia. It gained independence from Pakistan in 1971 after a nine-month-long war, which is commemorated on Victory Day, December 16th. The country is known for its lush green landscapes, the world’s longest sea beach at Cox’s Bazar, and the Sundarbans, the largest mangrove forest home to the Royal Bengal Tiger12.

Despite its small size, Bangladesh has a significant population and is one of the most densely populated countries in the world. The people are known for their hospitality and resilience. Economically, Bangladesh has made impressive strides, particularly in the garment industry, becoming one of the top exporters globally23.
